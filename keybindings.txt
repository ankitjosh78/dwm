Win + Shift + Enter -->pcmanfm(file manager)

Ctrl + Alt + f -->Firefox

Win + p -->dmenu

Win + q -->kill application window

Win + Shift + d -->discord

Win + Shift + b -->Brave

Win + <F12> --> rofi launcher

Win + Shift + s -->Spotify

Win + Shift + c -->clementine

Win + Shift + o -->obs

Win + Shift + n-->nitrogen(wallpaper selector)
